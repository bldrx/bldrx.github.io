Welcome to BLDRX

BLDRX is a new company whose focus is to make builders' lives easier and more profitable by saving you time and money. We believe that a lot of money is needlessly spent on material purchases due to the lack of time to compare multiple suppliers.

Our premise is that getting quotes from local suppliers and then comparing them in detail is extremely time consuming and that a lot of money is being left on the table at the end. BLDRX has a plan to make this process simple, quick and painless by providing a method to take material lists and provide quick price comparisons from the local building suppliers in your area.
